Conditional Probability

$P(A\mid B)=\dfrac{P(A\cap B)}{P(B)}$
$P(A\cap B)=P(A)\cdot P(B\mid A)$

Independence & Exclusivity

Independent $\Leftrightarrow P(A\cap B)=P(A)\cdot P(B)$
Mut. exclusive $\Leftrightarrow P(A\cap B)=0$

Addition & Complement

$P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B)$
$P(A')=1-P(A)$

Total Probability

$P(A)=P(A\mid B)P(B)+P(A\mid B')P(B')$
$P(B\mid A)=\dfrac{P(A\mid B)P(B)}{P(A\mid B)P(B)+P(A\mid B')P(B')}$

Determinant & Inverse

$\det\begin{pmatrix}a&b\\c&d\end{pmatrix}=ad-bc$
$A^{-1}=\dfrac{1}{\det A}\,\text{adj}(A)$;  $\text{adj}(A)=C^T$ (transpose of cofactors)
Cofactor: $C_{ij}=(-1)^{i+j}M_{ij}$

System Solutions

Singular $\Leftrightarrow\det(A)=0$
$AX=B\Rightarrow X=A^{-1}B$
Row $[0\;0\;0\mid k],\;k\neq0$ → inconsistent (no solution)
Zero row in $A$ only → infinitely many solutions

Properties

$(AB)^T=B^TA^T$
$|AB|=|A||B|$
$|A^T|=|A|$

Integrating Factor

$\dfrac{dy}{dx}+P(x)y=Q(x)$
$\mu=e^{\int P(x)\,dx}$,   $\dfrac{d}{dx}(\mu y)=\mu Q(x)$

Separable

$\dfrac{dy}{dx}=f(x)g(y)\implies\int\dfrac{dy}{g(y)}=\int f(x)\,dx$

Useful Integrals

$\int e^{ax}\sin bx\,dx=\dfrac{e^{ax}(a\sin bx-b\cos bx)}{a^2+b^2}+C$
$\int e^{ax}\cos bx\,dx=\dfrac{e^{ax}(a\cos bx+b\sin bx)}{a^2+b^2}+C$

Auxiliary Equation

For $ay''+by'+cy=f(x)$, solve $am^2+bm+c=0$
Real distinct $m_1,m_2$: $y_c=Ae^{m_1x}+Be^{m_2x}$
Repeated $m$: $y_c=(A+Bx)e^{mx}$
Complex $\alpha\pm\beta i$: $y_c=e^{\alpha x}(A\cos\beta x+B\sin\beta x)$

Particular Integral Trials

$ke^{ax}$ → try $\lambda e^{ax}$
poly degree $n$ → try full poly of same degree
$a\sin bx+c\cos bx$ → try $\lambda\sin bx+\mu\cos bx$

General Solution

$y=y_c\;(\text{CF})+y_p\;(\text{PI})$

Permutations & Combinations

$^nP_r=\dfrac{n!}{(n-r)!}$
$^nC_r=\dbinom{n}{r}=\dfrac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}$

Arrangements

Repeated items: $\dfrac{n!}{n_1!\,n_2!\cdots}$
Together → treat as 1 block × internal arrangements
NOT together → Total − Together

Counting

AND → multiply  |  OR → add

Modulus-Argument

$z=r(\cos\theta+i\sin\theta)=re^{i\theta}$
$|z|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}$,   $z\bar{z}=|z|^2\in\mathbb{R}$
$|\bar{z}|=|z|$,   $\arg(\bar{z})=-\arg(z)$

De Moivre & Locus

$(\cos\theta+i\sin\theta)^n=\cos n\theta+i\sin n\theta$
$|z-a|=r$ → circle centre $a$, radius $r$
$\arg(z-a)=\theta$ → half-line from $a$ at angle $\theta$

Maclaurin Series

$e^x=1+x+\dfrac{x^2}{2!}+\dfrac{x^3}{3!}+\cdots$
$\sin x=x-\dfrac{x^3}{3!}+\dfrac{x^5}{5!}-\cdots$
$\cos x=1-\dfrac{x^2}{2!}+\dfrac{x^4}{4!}-\cdots$
$(1+x)^n=1+nx+\dfrac{n(n-1)}{2!}x^2+\cdots,\;|x|<1$

Series & Key Calculus

$S_\infty=\dfrac{a}{1-r},\;|r|<1$
$\displaystyle\sum_{r=1}^n r=\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}$
$\displaystyle\int\frac{dx}{1+a^2x^2}=\frac{1}{a}\tan^{-1}(ax)+C$
Newton-Raphson: $x_{n+1}=x_n-\dfrac{f(x_n)}{f'(x_n)}$

Quadratic & Binomial

$x=\dfrac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$
$(a+b)^n=\displaystyle\sum_{r=0}^n\binom{n}{r}a^{n-r}b^r$
$(1+x)^n=1+nx+\dfrac{n(n-1)}{2!}x^2+\cdots,\;|x|<1,\;n\in\mathbb{R}$

Summations

$\displaystyle\sum_{r=1}^n r=\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}$
$\displaystyle\sum_{r=1}^n r^2=\dfrac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$
$\displaystyle\sum_{r=1}^n r^3=\left[\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}\right]^2$

Complex Numbers

$z^n=r^n(\cos n\theta+i\sin n\theta)$
$e^{ix}=\cos x+i\sin x$

Question 2
Isaiah's Lunch Decision 🍱
12 marks
Every school day, Isaiah goes to the canteen with probability $\dfrac{2}{5}$ and to Nikhal's roadside stand with probability $\dfrac{3}{5}$. If he goes to the canteen, the probability he is satisfied with his meal is $\dfrac{3}{4}$. If he goes to Nikhal's stand, the probability he is satisfied is $\dfrac{9}{10}$.
  • (a) Draw a tree diagram to represent this information, showing all probabilities on the branches and at each end node.
    [3]
  • (b) Find the probability that on a randomly chosen school day Isaiah is satisfied with his lunch.

  • (a) Write the information above as a system of three linear equations and express it in the matrix form $AX = B$, clearly stating the matrices $A$, $X$ and $B$.

    [3]
